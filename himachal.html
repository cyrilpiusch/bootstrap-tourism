Shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is one of India’s most popular hill stations. Known for its colonial architecture, the Ridge, and Mall Road, Shimla is also surrounded by beautiful scenic landscapes of snow-capped mountains. It’s a hub for winter sports and trekking.

Manali

Manali is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Beas River Valley. It offers an array of adventure sports like skiing, paragliding, and trekking. Popular spots include Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, and Hidimba Temple. Manali also serves as a base for exploring the nearby Leh-Ladakh.

Spiti Valley

Spiti Valley is an off-the-beaten-path destination known for its barren beauty, monasteries, and mountain ranges. It is home to the Key Monastery, Chandratal Lake, and Tabo Monastery (often called the "Ajanta of the Himalayas" for its ancient frescoes). Spiti is perfect for those looking for a serene and adventurous escape into nature.
