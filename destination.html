STATES OF INDIA

Andhra Pradesh

Famous for its rich culture, beaches, and the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple.
Kerala

Known for its backwaters, Ayurvedic treatments, and rich cultural heritage.
Rajasthan

Known for its royal history, forts, palaces, and the Thar Desert.
Sikkim

A small state in the Himalayas, famous for its natural beauty and Buddhist monasteries.
Goa

Renowned for its beautiful beaches, Portuguese culture, and vibrant nightlife.
Himachal Pradesh

Known for its beautiful hill stations, like Manali and Shimla, and trekking routes.
Arunachal Pradesh

Known for its scenic beauty and numerous tribal cultures in the Northeast.
Assam

Famous for its tea gardens and the Kaziranga National Park.

Bihar

A state with rich historical and religious significance, especially Buddhist sites.

Chhattisgarh

Known for its natural resources, waterfalls, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Gujarat

Home to the Rann of Kutch, the Gir National Park, and the Dwarkadhish Temple.

Haryana

Known for its agricultural wealth and the Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary.

Jharkhand

A state rich in minerals, forests, and famous for the Ranchi Falls.

Karnataka

Famous for its royal history, Bangalore's tech hub, and the UNESCO-listed Hampi.

Madhya Pradesh

Known for its ancient temples, UNESCO World Heritage Sites, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Maharashtra

Famous for Mumbai, the gateway of India, and rich history of forts and beaches.

Manipur

Known for its unique culture, dance forms, and beautiful lakes.

Meghalaya

Known for its breathtaking hills, caves, and the cleanest village in Asia, Mawlynnong.

Mizoram

Known for its hilly terrain, and a rich blend of culture and traditions.

Nagaland

Famous for its vibrant festivals, traditional tribal cultures, and scenic landscapes.

Odisha

Known for its temples, beaches, and the famous Rath Yatra festival in Puri.

Punjab

Famous for its golden temple, vibrant culture, and agriculture.

Tamil Nadu

Known for its ancient temples, classical dance forms, and beautiful coastlines.

Telangana

Famous for its historical sites, food culture, and the city of Hyderabad.

Tripura

A state rich in culture, folklore, and beautiful landscapes.

Uttar Pradesh

Home to the Taj Mahal, historical cities, and religious sites like Varanasi.

Uttarakhand

Known for its pilgrimage sites and hill stations like Nainital and Mussoorie.

West Bengal

Famous for its rich culture, Durga Puja, and the coastal city of Kolkata.
