Tawang

Tawang is one of Arunachal Pradesh’s most famous tourist destinations, known for the Tawang Monastery, the largest Buddhist monastery in India. The town is nestled in the Himalayan range and offers stunning landscapes, including beautiful lakes like Sela Pass and Madhuri Lake. Tawang is also rich in Tibetan culture.

Ziro Valley

Ziro Valley is famous for its lush green landscapes, rice fields, and picturesque villages like Apatanis. This UNESCO World Heritage tentative site is also known for its vibrant cultural festivals, such as the Ziro Festival of Music. The valley is perfect for trekking, bird watching, and experiencing local traditions.

Namdapha National Park

Namdapha National Park, located in the eastern part of Arunachal Pradesh, is one of India’s largest and most biodiverse national parks. It is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including tigers, leopards, and snow leopards, as well as unique plant species. The park offers trekking and wildlife safaris.
