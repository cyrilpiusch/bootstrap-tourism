Gangtok

Gangtok is the vibrant capital of Sikkim, located amidst the Eastern Himalayas, offering breathtaking views of Mount Kanchenjunga, cultural experiences, and adventure activities.

Pelling

Pelling is a scenic town offering some of the best views of the Kanchenjunga range, with lush green landscapes and historic monasteries.

Yuksom

Yuksom is a small town and the gateway to trekking routes in Sikkim. It is rich in history and culture and offers an incredible experience for nature lovers.
